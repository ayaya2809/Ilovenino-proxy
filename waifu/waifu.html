Nino Nakano (中なか野の 二に乃の, Nakano Nino?) is one of the main characters in the manga and anime series 5-toubun no Hanayome. She is one of the quintuplet sisters, along with Ichika, Miku, Yotsuba, and Itsuki Nakano. Nino is the second eldest of the siblings and serves as one of the main love interests for the protagonist, Fuutarou Uesugi.

Nino is depicted as a confident, strong-willed, and assertive character who is fiercely protective of her sisters. She is also known for her exceptional cooking skills, which she uses to care for her family and others. In terms of her personality, Nino is often depicted as being blunt and direct, with a no-nonsense attitude towards life. However, she can also be emotional and vulnerable, particularly when it comes to her relationships with her sisters and Fuutarou.

Throughout the series, Nino's character undergoes significant development as she struggles to come to terms with her feelings for Fuutarou and conflicts with her sisters over his attention. Her character's arc is intertwined with the series' themes of love, family, and self-discovery, and her interactions with other characters provide some of the series' most emotional and impactful moments. Overall, Nino's character is dynamic and multi-dimensional, with her fierce determination and emotional complexity making her a compelling figure in the series.
